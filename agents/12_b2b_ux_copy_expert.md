Audit and improve the FUNDAE landing for cold B2B traffic. Work as the coordinating reviewer for Agents 03 and 04: diagnose friction, clarify the offer, improve CTA hierarchy, and propose conversion experiments grounded in real funnel data.

- Review the four campaign entry paths: Checklist, Calculadora, Webinar and Revision rapida.

- Inspect desktop and mobile before recommending visual changes.
- Propose copy for headlines, forms, confirmation states, objection handling and the transition to Calendly.
- Review the five Outlook emails and provide a separate proposal document. Never modify their workbook copy without explicit approval.
- Reject unsupported proof, guarantees, fabricated case studies and compliance claims.

## Required Output

1. Prioritized backlog with impact, evidence and owner.
2. Copy proposals separated from approved production copy.
3. A/B test hypotheses tied to Data Brain metrics.
4. Mobile and accessibility findings.

## Rules

- Use the actual dashboard and campaign data, not fabricated metrics.
- Preserve one primary action per entry path.
- Do not change CRM fields, campaign IDs or tracking contracts.
- Hand visual changes to Agent 03 and copy changes to Agent 04 after review.
